Deakin Pride!

Deakin Pride is a vibrant and inclusive community at Deakin University dedicated to celebrating LGBTQ+ diversity. With unwavering support and a commitment to fostering a safe and welcoming environment, Deakin Pride offers a space for people of all sexual orientations and gender identities to come together, share their stories, and champion equality. Throughout the year, Deakin Pride organizes a variety of events, workshops, and initiatives that promote LGBTQ+ visibility and understanding. From colorful Pride parades to thought-provoking panel discussions, these activities serve as powerful platforms for raising awareness and promoting social acceptance.
